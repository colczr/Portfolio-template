  • Mend


    Mobile App Design / UX Design

Mend is a health app that aims to improves communication between patients with mental conditions and their therapist and health provider. Mend provides daily logs for the patient to record their mood, diary and a variety of measures to share with the therapist. The therapist can customize the content for the patient and utilize the data collected from the patient to better customize the therapy as well as discovering problems in an efficient manner.

Problems


Therapists

· Therapists only see clients one hour per week. That is 1 hour out of 168 hours.
· Therapists can’t get a thorough understanding of what happened in their clients’ lives.
· Therapists can’t makesure that the clients are actually doing their homework.
· Traditional paper-pencil assessments are hard to analyze after being collected.

Patients

· Patients don’t have enough time to tell their stories.
· Patients often forget things and don’t know what’s important to mention during a session.
· Patients forget to do homework sometimes.

Solution

· Customizable assessment content and frequency of assessment set by therapist.
· Automatically reminds client to complete assignments.
· Generates weekly reports to therapist for planning tailored and focused therapy sessions.
· Flags potentially dangerous situations based on assessments and notifies therapist.
